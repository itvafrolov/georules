    Intelligent Geospatial Economics

    INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGIES FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF TERRITORIES



    Intelligent geospatial economy is a new innovation and investment product with the application of modern digital and geospatial technologies, mathematical modeling, artificial intelligence which takes into account spatial, urban planning, investment, infrastructure, social and other territorial particularities. Intelligent geospatial economy provides an increase in the efficiency of use of territories, allows to attract investment resources, develop and implement strategies for territorial development, execute infrastructure, social and other projects. The International Association for the Sustainable Development of Territories unites partners and provides interaction between foreign and domestic stakeholders to solve issues on sustainable development of territories.
